    About Me

    Hello! My name is Abigail Johnson and I am a recent graduate of Dartmouth College, where I studied Quantitative Social Science and Human-Centered Design. Design gave me the mindset and the tools to tackle complex, intangible problems with a sense of agency, and it's my goal to apply my design skills to the things I love. I spent the last 16 months applying design thinking and my passion for equitable access to outdoor recreation at the National Park Service, where I supported adaptive recreation in the parks through resource, training, and program development. In January 2025, I'm heading to Sweden on the H. Allen Brooks Travelling Fellowship to study the nuances of access to outdoor recreation in Nordic countries.
